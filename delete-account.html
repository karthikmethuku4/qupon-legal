Qupon – Delete Account & Data

⚠️ Important: Requesting account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read all information below before proceeding.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Qupon account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at businessqupon@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Phone Number or Email]"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your registered phone number or email address
    • Full name associated with your account
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  4. Alternative method: Call our Customer Care at +91 9121289189 and request account deletion (you will need to verify your identity)
  5. Verification: Our team will verify your identity before processing the deletion request
  6. Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account deletion is initiated

Processing Time

Upon receiving and verifying your deletion request, Qupon will delete your account and associated personal data within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, Qupon will permanently delete the following information:

  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile information and user settings
  • Payment details (bank account/UPI information if linked)
  • Uploaded coupon data
  • Purchase history
  • Account preferences and settings
  • All user-generated content

What Data May Be Retained

In certain circumstances, Qupon may be required to retain certain data for legal, regulatory, or operational purposes:

  • Legal obligations: Transaction records required by law (e.g., financial regulations, tax compliance) may be retained as mandated by applicable Indian laws
  • Dispute resolution: Data related to ongoing disputes, investigations, or legal proceedings may be retained until resolution
  • Security purposes: Logs related to security incidents or fraud prevention may be retained for a limited period as required by law
  • Aggregated analytics: Non-personal, anonymized, aggregated data used for analytics purposes (does not identify you individually)

Any retained data will be kept only for the minimum period required by law and will not be used for any other purpose.

Additional Retention Period

If data must be retained for legal or regulatory compliance, it will be kept for the minimum period required by applicable Indian laws, typically not exceeding 7 years from the date of account deletion, after which it will be permanently destroyed.

Before You Delete Your Account

Please consider the following:

  • Outstanding transactions: Complete any pending transactions or withdrawals before requesting deletion
  • Active coupons: If you have active coupon listings, they will be removed from the platform
  • Earnings: Ensure all earnings have been withdrawn to your linked bank account or UPI
  • Irreversible action: Account deletion cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Qupon again in the future
